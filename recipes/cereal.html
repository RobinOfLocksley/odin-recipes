Cereal Guide

It's 7am in the morning and you've woken up late.

You need to leave at 7:30 and after a speedy shower and change you only have 10 minutes to eat breakfast. Luckily with this 20 minute guide there is a meal you can have.

First, prepwork

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal of your choice
  • Bowl (optional)
  • Spoon (optional)

Now comes the "hard part"

Instructions

  1. Grab the cereal of your choice
  2. Now go and get the Milk
  3. If you have a bowl, pour the cereal in the bowl
    • If you want to put milk in first then you can. However, everyone is judging you.

      • For the purpose of these instructions, we'll continue as if you're normal

  4. If you have a bowl, now put a normal amount of milk to cereal ratio.

    5. if you don't have a bowl, just pour the cereal and milk in your mouth and God help you.

  5. Now enjoy with the spoon of your choice.
